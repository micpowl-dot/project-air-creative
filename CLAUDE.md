## Anti-Patterns — Do Not Do These

- Do not use corporate blue, tech-startup teal, or gradient blobs in the visual system
- Do not reference team member names in the presentation deck (Mark does not want the sausage-making visible)
- Do not use circuit boards, neural network nodes, or glowing orbs as AI illustration metaphors
